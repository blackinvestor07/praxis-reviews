Which should you choose?

Choose Systeme.io if you're a one-person business or small team running a course, coaching practice, dropshipping store, or content brand and you want one platform doing everything — funnels, emails, courses, memberships, automation, and your own affiliate program — for one monthly fee. Especially if you're starting out and need a free way to test before paying anything.

Choose GetResponse if your business is primarily email-driven and you need sophisticated automation, conditional workflows, and native webinars. Best fit if you already use Shopify or WooCommerce and want deep ecommerce email integration, or if running live webinars is part of how you sell.

Common questions

Is the Systeme.io free plan really free, or just a trial in disguise?
It's genuinely free, forever — no credit card required at signup, no automatic conversion to a paid plan, no time limit. The free tier includes 2,000 contacts, 3 sales funnels, 1 online course, 1 membership site, and 10,000 emails per month. You only pay if you want to lift those limits or unlock advanced features (more funnels, unlimited emails, evergreen webinars). Many Indian creators run their entire side business on the free plan for months before upgrading.
Can I receive payments from my Indian customers in INR?
Yes, with both platforms. Systeme.io integrates with Stripe (which now supports INR receipts via Stripe India) and PayPal — your customers can pay using credit cards, debit cards, UPI (through Stripe), or PayPal balances. GetResponse handles payments through the same processors. Neither has direct Razorpay integration as of this review, but Stripe India covers the same use cases including UPI checkout on web.
How does this compare to Indian alternatives like Convertful, LeadSquared, or Cake?
The Indian-built alternatives serve specific niches — LeadSquared is CRM-led (sales teams, not creators), Convertful focuses on lead capture popups, and Cake leans into B2B marketing automation. Systeme.io and GetResponse are a different category: end-to-end creator-economy platforms covering funnels, courses, memberships, and email together. If you're selling online courses, coaching, info-products, or dropshipping, the all-in-one international tools fit better. If you're running a sales team or B2B pipeline, look at the Indian options.
Can I cancel anytime? Are there long contracts?
Both work month-to-month with no contract — cancel anytime from your account dashboard. Annual plans are discounted (Systeme.io ~30% off, GetResponse ~18% off) and are refundable in full within the first 30 days. After 30 days, an annual plan can still be cancelled but the prorated balance isn't refunded — so start monthly, switch to annual only after you've validated the platform fits your business.
