Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Praxis Reviews collects when you visit praxisreviews.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1 · Who we are

Praxis Reviews ("we," "us," "our") operates praxisreviews.com, an independent software review and comparison site based in Montreal, Canada. This policy covers the information we collect through this website.

2 · Information we collect

Analytics data. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site — pages viewed, time on page, device and browser type, approximate location (city/country level, derived from IP address), and referral source. This data is aggregated and is not used by us to identify you personally.

Cookies. Our site uses cookies and similar technologies (such as browser local storage) set by us and by third-party services like Google Analytics, to remember basic preferences and to measure site usage. See Section 3 for details.

Information you provide directly. If you contact us through our contact page or email us, we collect whatever information you include — typically your name, email address, and message content — solely to respond to you.

We do not require account creation to browse this site, and we do not knowingly collect payment card details, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal information.

3 · Cookies and tracking technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for:

  • Analytics — Google Analytics (GA4) uses cookies to distinguish visitors and measure site performance.
  • Advertising — if we run or later run advertising campaigns (for example, Google Ads), conversion and remarketing cookies may be used to measure ad performance and, where applicable, show relevant ads to past visitors on other sites.
  • Affiliate tracking — when you click a link to a partner product, the partner (or their affiliate network) may set a cookie to track that the visit came from Praxis Reviews, so we can be credited for a resulting sale. This is standard practice for affiliate marketing and is covered further in our affiliate disclosure.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect how some parts of the site function, but the site will remain readable. You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites using Google's browser opt-out add-on.

4 · Advertising

We may run paid advertising (such as Google Ads) to promote pages on this site. Advertising platforms may use cookies and similar technologies to measure ad performance and avoid showing you the same ad repeatedly. We do not share personally identifying information with advertising platforms beyond what those platforms collect directly through their own tracking pixels or tags.

5 · Third-party links

Our site contains links to third-party products and services, many of which are affiliate links (see our affiliate disclosure). Once you leave our site, that third party's own privacy policy governs any information you provide to them. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

6 · How we use and share information

We use the information described above to operate and improve the site, understand which content is useful to readers, and respond to messages sent through our contact page. We do not sell personal information. We share data only with the service providers who help us run the site (such as Google Analytics and our hosting provider), and only as needed for them to provide that service.

7 · Data retention

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings. Messages sent through our contact page are kept only as long as needed to respond and to maintain a reasonable record of correspondence.

8 · Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, or to ask a question about this policy, contact us. You can also manage cookie preferences directly in your browser at any time.

9 · Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general business and adult audience and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10 · Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as our practices or applicable law change. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the revised policy.

11 · Contact us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled can be sent through our contact page.
