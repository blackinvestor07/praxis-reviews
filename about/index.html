About Praxis Reviews

Software buying guides for emerging markets — written by people who actually use the tools.

Praxis Reviews is an independent review site based in Montreal, Canada. We publish software buying guides for people building businesses in emerging markets — India, Indonesia, and other fast-growing regions where the "best" tool for a US audience often isn't the best pick locally, once you factor in local payment support, price sensitivity, and what's actually available.

Our mission is simple: help solopreneurs, creators, and small business owners choose software with confidence, without wading through marketing pages designed to obscure the tradeoffs. We compare real alternatives head-to-head, using the workflows an actual business owner would run, not a checklist copied from a press release.

We're reader-supported. When you buy through some of our links, we may earn a commission — see our testing methodology and affiliate disclosure for exactly how that works and how it does (and doesn't) affect what we recommend.
